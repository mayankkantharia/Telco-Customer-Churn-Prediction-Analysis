Original (5 bullets)

- Translated model insights into business actions, prioritizing retention for high-bill/short-tenure and month-to-month segments and recommending billing method shifts to reduce churn risk.
- Produced a reproducible analysis package with separate EDA and modeling notebooks, model comparison tables, and documented methodology/results for portfolio-ready presentation.
